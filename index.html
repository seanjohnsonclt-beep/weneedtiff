What we built

The retention layer
medicine was missing.

Adherix is an SMS-first behavioral engine. It runs silently alongside any GLP-1 program — no app, no EHR integration, no new workflow for clinical staff. It just keeps patients on therapy.

01
Detect drift early
Behavioral signals — silence, missed replies, keyword patterns — trigger automated intervention before a patient quits. We catch them at week 3, not week 12.
02
Act with precision
Phase-based SMS sequences. Directive, warm, short. Not a chatbot. Not a coach. A quietly intelligent system that knows what week a patient is on and what they need to hear.
03
Optimize the cohort
Every clinic gets a real-time retention dashboard. Recovered patients, flagged patients, revenue protected. The kind of data that turns a pilot into a permanent line item.
