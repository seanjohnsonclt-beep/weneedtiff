The company

GLP-1 drugs work.
Patients don't stay on them.

Semaglutide - Ozempic, Wegovy - is the most effective weight-loss treatment ever discovered. It works. But over 60% of patients quit within six months. Not because the drug stopped working. Because nobody was there to help them stay.

Side effects hit. A shot gets skipped. Life gets in the way. And the clinic has no idea until the patient just... stops showing up.

That's the gap. And that's what Adherix Health closes.

Adherix is a behavior engine that keeps GLP-1 patients on treatment longer - through SMS, timing, and behavioral triggers. Not an app. Not a dashboard. Just the right message at the right moment.
How it works

The right text. At the right time.

Adherix runs silently in the background. Every patient gets a personalized sequence of SMS messages timed to their phase of treatment, dose schedule, and timezone. When something goes quiet, the system notices.
